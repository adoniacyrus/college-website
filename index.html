About Us


St. Antony’s College Peruvanthanam was founded in 2013 with the aim of nurturing and grooming students to become capable architects of future society by imparting knowledge, understanding, skills, and critical thinking. At SAC, we strive to educate young aspiring minds by providing an academic environment and innovative pedagogy that helps them become versatile individuals. Our goal is to prepare students to succeed in a highly competitive professional environment and become expert professionals themselves.

This eco-friendly institution with evergreen scenery blows our mind by its serenity and purity. Our Academic wing constitutes 11 UG Courses along with PG Programme. The innovative and New Generation courses that create opportunities in the global areas such as BSc Cyber Forensic , B.Com Professional with ACCA , B.Com Professional CA/ CMA (India), and BFT are excellently functioning here. B.Com professional with CMA (US) and MBA ( IGNOU ) which are proposed to start soon really empower and equip the students. To earn a safe and secure future , we conduct campus placement and Recruitment drives for the students. We focus on imparting quality education and ample opportunities to students and aims to make them successful in life.
